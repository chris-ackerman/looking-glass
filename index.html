Looking Glass

The Looking Glass: Revolutionize the way you look at yourself. The next iteration in connected smart home technology.

“The first appearance deceives many; intelligence perceives what has been carefully hidden”- Phaedrus


As you take a closer look at The Looking Glass, you can see that behind the two-way reflective panel is the LED display. Behind the LED display is what powers the smart mirror, the Raspberry Pi. It is a small, credit card sized computer with the capability to browse the internet, access spreadsheets, and much more.

Seamless connection via our IOS or Web application to have total control over your mirror.

Plug in your mirror, configure it the way you like, and let us do all of the software changes to keep you up to date.

Built on the Magic Mirror open source platform, you have 150+ options to configure your mirror any way you'd like.

  • With our IOS application, you have the power of our proprietary software at your fingertips.

  • Scroll through the 150+ configuration options and select which items you'd like to display on your mirror.

  • Simply enter your account information for that specific item and let our servers do the rest of the work for you.
